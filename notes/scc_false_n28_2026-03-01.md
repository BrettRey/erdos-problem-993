## Strategic Implications

1. **SCC is too strong** as an intermediate target. It fails at n=28 while E >= J holds.
2. **The entire B1+B2 decomposition infrastructure** was built on SCC >= 0. This is now obsolete.
3. **The W-form** (J_k * Delta_k(A,C) + J_{k+1} * d_{k-1}(B,C) >= 0) remains valid and is the right proof target.
4. **E >= J is WEAKER than SCC** and remains computationally clean.
5. The proof must go directly: E >= J via W-form induction, NOT through SCC.
