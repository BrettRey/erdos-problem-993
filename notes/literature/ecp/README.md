# Recent ECP comparator corpus

Retrieved 2026-07-16 for the proposed submission of *A variance-normalized curvature bound at the first descent of Poisson--binomial laws* to *Electronic Communications in Probability* (ECP).

Each PDF has a companion plain-text extraction produced with `pdftotext -layout`. Files ending in `_preprint` are open arXiv versions of papers subsequently published in ECP. Project Euclid's official PDF endpoints returned an Incapsula challenge to automated requests; the ECP DOI and official article metadata were nevertheless verified. The two files without `_preprint` are publisher PDFs obtained from institutional repositories.
